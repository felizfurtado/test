My Healing Journey

For over a decade, I've guided clients toward emotional freedom and spiritual awakening through compassionate therapy and intuitive wisdom.

My Story

My path to becoming a therapist began during my own healing journey. After experiencing the transformative power of holistic therapy firsthand, I knew I wanted to help others find that same light within themselves.

I trained at the California Institute of Integral Studies, blending traditional psychotherapy with spiritual practices. What emerged was a unique approach that honors both science and soul.

When I'm not working with clients, you'll find me tending to my herb garden, painting, or practicing yoga. These creative outlets fuel my therapeutic work and keep me grounded.
